THE PROCESS

Every project follows this exact flow. No surprises.

01
Brief & Discovery

You fill out a short questionnaire (or we chat). I ask about your goals, target audience, competitors, and what success looks like for you. Duration: 1 day.

02
Quote & Agreement

I send a clear breakdown of deliverables, timeline, and cost. Once agreed, I send the invoice for the 50% deposit. Work begins on payment.

03
Design & Development

I build the project in stages. You get a first draft / preview at the midpoint. Feedback is collected, revisions are made. No ghosting. No surprises.

04
Review & Refine

You review the work and provide feedback. We go through your included revision rounds until everything is exactly as you want it.

05
Final Delivery

Final 50% payment due. On receipt I deliver all files, handover documentation, and any deployment assistance needed. Project closed — but I stay reachable.

WHAT I NEED FROM YOU

To start quickly and deliver well, I’ll need these from you:

  • Your brief or vision — What are you building, who is it for, and what do you want people to feel when they see it?
  • Any existing brand assets — Logos, fonts, color codes, previous designs — whatever you have, even if rough.
  • Website content or copy — If it’s a website, I’ll need the text, headings, and any images you want to use.
  • Reference or inspiration — Links to sites or brands you like. Helps me understand your taste quickly.
  • Feedback within 48 hours — The faster you review drafts, the faster the project moves. Long waits push timelines.
  • The 50% deposit — Work starts immediately on payment. I hold your project slot once confirmed.
