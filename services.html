FAQ

How do payments work? +
50% deposit upfront, 50% on delivery. Larger projects can use milestone payments. I accept bank transfer, PayPal, and other agreed methods.
How long does a project take? +
Simple landing page: 5–7 days. Full website: 2–4 weeks. Brand identity: 7–14 days. Timeline depends on feedback speed — the faster you review, the faster we deliver.
What do you need from me to start? +
A brief (or I’ll send a questionnaire), any existing brand assets, content/copy if it’s a website, and the deposit. I’ll guide you through the rest.
Do you work with international clients? +
Yes, fully remote and open worldwide. I’ve worked with clients across Nigeria, the UK, US, and other countries. Time zones are manageable with good communication.
What if I’m not happy with the result? +
Every package includes revision rounds. I don’t close a project until you’re satisfied. If there’s a major issue we’ll talk through it honestly and find a solution.
Do you hand over source files? +
Yes. On completion you receive everything — CorelDRAW originals, Figma files, full source code, all exported assets. Everything belongs to you once payment is complete.
